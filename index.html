WHO WE ARE
"Starbucks Coffee Indonesia opens its first outlet in Plaza Indonesia, 17 May 2002. As of January 2018, Starbucks Coffee Indonesia is now located in 326 different locations across major cities (22 cities), connecting one cup to Indonesians at a time. Starbucks sells hot and cold drinks, coffee beans, salads, hot and cold sandwiches, sweet pastries, snacks, and items such as glasses and tumblers. Through the Starbucks Entertainment division and the Hear Music brand, the company also markets books, music and films."
